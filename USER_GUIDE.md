# Quick Start Guide for Adding Recipes

This guide will help you add recipes to Pot Shot Society without any coding!

## Step 1: Access the Admin Panel

1. Open your browser and go to: **`yoursite.com/studio`**
   - (When testing locally, use `localhost:3000/studio`)
2. Log in with your email and password

## Step 2: Create a New Recipe

1. Click the **"Create"** button in the top navigation
2. Select **"Recipe"** from the menu

## Step 3: Fill Out the Recipe Form

### Required Fields (marked with *)

**Recipe Title*** 
- The name of your dish
- Example: "Mom's Chocolate Chip Cookies"

**URL Slug***
- Click the **"Generate"** button next to this field
- It will automatically create a URL-friendly version of your title

**Main Recipe Photo***
- Drag and drop a photo, or click to select from your computer
- Use a clear, appetizing photo of the finished dish

**Ingredients***
- Click **"+ Add ingredient"** for each ingredient
- Write complete ingredient lines (e.g., "2 cups all-purpose flour")
- You can drag items to reorder them

**Instructions***
- Click **"+ Add step"** for each instruction
- Write one step per box
- You can drag steps to reorder them

**Categories***
- Select at least one category from the dropdown
- You can select multiple categories

### Optional Fields

**Short Description**
- A 1-2 sentence overview of the recipe
- Example: "These classic chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible."

**Additional Photos**
- Add extra photos showing process steps or different angles
- Click **"+ Add"** to upload more images

**Prep Time (minutes)**
- How long it takes to prepare ingredients
- Enter just the number (e.g., 15)

**Cook Time (minutes)**
- How long it takes to cook/bake
- Enter just the number (e.g., 12)

**Number of Servings**
- How many servings the recipe makes
- Example: 24 (for 24 cookies)

**Recipe Notes & Tips**
- Storage instructions, variations, substitutions
- Example: "These cookies freeze well for up to 3 months!"

**Recipe Source**
- Where the recipe came from
- Example: "Grandma's handwritten cookbook"

## Step 4: Publish Your Recipe

1. Review all the information
2. Click the **"Publish"** button in the bottom right
3. Your recipe is now live! It will appear on the website within 1-2 minutes

## Step 5: Share Your Recipe

1. Go to your website homepage
2. Find your recipe in the grid
3. Click on it to open the full recipe page
4. Click the **"Share"** button to copy the URL
5. Paste the URL in a text message, email, or social media to share with friends!

## Tips for Great Recipe Photos

- Use natural light when possible
- Take photos from a slight angle (not directly overhead)
- Include garnishes or props for visual interest
- Make sure the food is the focus of the photo

## Editing an Existing Recipe

1. Go to the Studio (`yoursite.com/studio`)
2. You'll see a list of all your recipes
3. Click on the recipe you want to edit
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Publish"** to save

## Deleting a Recipe

1. Open the recipe in the Studio
2. Click the menu button (three dots) in the top right
3. Select **"Delete"**
4. Confirm the deletion

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does it take for my recipe to appear on the site?**
A: About 1-2 minutes after you click "Publish"

**Q: Can I save a recipe as a draft?**
A: Yes! Just don't click "Publish". The recipe will be saved as a draft that only you can see in the Studio.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: No problem! Just edit the recipe and publish again. Your changes will appear within 1-2 minutes.

**Q: Can I add multiple photos?**
A: Yes! Use the "Additional Photos" field to add as many photos as you want.

**Q: What categories should I choose?**
A: Choose the categories that best describe your recipe. You can select multiple! Available categories:
- Breakfast & Brunch
- Appetizers & Snacks
- Main Dishes
- Side Dishes
- Soups & Salads
- Desserts
- Breads & Baking
- Drinks & Beverages
- Sauces & Condiments
- Quick & Easy
- Vegetarian
- Kid-Friendly

## Need Help?

If you run into any issues, contact the person who set up the site for you, and they can help troubleshoot!

Happy cooking and sharing!
